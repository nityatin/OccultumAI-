Here is a list of spells that Harry Potter has cast over the course of 7 Books and 8 Movies in Alphabetical Order. I have not included spells from the games, Cursed Child or anywhere outside of the Books and Movies I have only included spells we have actually seen him and not ones that he should be able to use (spells taught to younger years like Immobulus) or ones was contemplating using (Feather Light Charm, Entomorphis, etc)


Accio (The Summoning Charm)

Aguamenti (The Water-Making Spell)

Alohomora (Unlocking Charm)

Arania Exumai (Spider repelling spell)

Ascendio (used to reach the surface in second task)

Cauldron to badger (transformation spell; used in OotP)

Cave inimicum (one of the protections cast in DH when the trio were on run)

Cheering Charm

Colloportus (Locking Spell)

Colovaria (Colour Change Charm)

Confringo (Blasting Curse)

Confundo (Confundus Charm)

Crinus Muto (Transfiguration to change hair color and style)

Crucio (Cruciatus Curse)

Defodio (Gouging Spell)

Depulso (Banishing Charm)

Descendo (used in RoR on a 50 foot pile of junk to stop Fiendfyre from following the trio)

Diffindo (Severing Charm)

Dissendium (used in DH pt 1 in an attempt to destroy the Locket)

Disillusionment Charm (one of the protections cast in DH when the trio were on run)

Engorgio (Engorgement Charm)

Episkey (used to heal Demelza Robins' broken lip)

Erecto (used to erect tents)

Evanesco (Vanishing Spell)

Expecto Patronum (Patronus Charm)

Expelliarmus (Disarming Charm)

Expulso (Expulso Curse; used in DH pt 1 in an attempt to destroy the locket)

Finite Incantatem (General Counter-Spell; used in RoR in DH)

Furnunculus (Pimple Jinx; used in GoF against Crabbe, and Goyle)

Glacius (Freezing Spell; used in HBP)

Growth Charm

Impedimenta (Impediment Jinx; used a lot on a lot of different enemies)

Imperio (Imperius Curse)

Impervius (Impervius Charm; used in OotP to practice in the rain)

Incarcerous (Incarcerous Spell; used against Inferi)

Incendio (Fire-Making Spell; used non verbally against the locket)

Langlock (Jinx to affix one's tongue to the roof of their mouth; used against Peeves in HBP)

Leg Sprouting Charm (pointed out by u/Carrot-Bro)

Levicorpus (used non verbally against Draco Malfoy in the bathroom duel)

Liberacorpus (used on himself to counteract the Levicorpus Hermione cast on him in DH)

Locomotor Mortis (Leg-Locker Curse; used non verbally against Draco Malfoy in the batroom duel)

Lumos (Wand-Lighting Charm)

Lumos Maxima

Muffliato (Muffliato Charm; one of the protections cast in DH when the trio were on run)

Nox (Wand-Extinguishing Charm)

Oppugno (Oppugno Jinx; used non verbally while escaping MoM in DH pt 1)

Periculum (used in GoF to send red sparks)

Petrificus Totalus (Full Body-Bind Curse)

Point Me (Four Point Spell; used in the third task)

Protego (Shield Charm; Harry's shown to have impressive mastery with this spell; going from it shattering from Hermione's Jelly Leg curse in GoF to surpassing Ministry Officials and being able to block curses from Voldemort)

Protego totalum (one of the protections cast in DH when the trio were on run)

Reducio (Shrinking Charm; used in DH pt 1)

Reducto (Reductor Curse)

Refilling Charm (used non verbally in HBP)

Relashio (Revulsion Jinx; used against Grindylows in GoF and in DH to release the dragon)

Rennervate (Reviving Spell)

Reparo (Mending Charm)

Repello Muggletum (Muggle-Repelling Charm; one of the protections cast in DH when the trio were on run)

Rictusempra (Tickling Charm)

Riddikulus (Boggart Banishing Spell)
